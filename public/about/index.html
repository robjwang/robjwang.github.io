About Me#

I lead the GTM Data Science team at Stripe. Previously, I’ve worked on data science teams (in both managerial and individual contributor capacities) at Block (Square), Robinhood Markets, Slack Technologies, and Airbnb, developing broad expertise across growth/GTM, online marketplace, SaaS, payments, and fraud detection domains.
